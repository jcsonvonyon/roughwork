LandCrowdy is Nigeria’s 1st Property tech company whose mission is to end the problem of shelter in Nigeria, one building at a time. We do this by creating an online platform for “everyday people'', to engage in the real estate value chain by building affordable homes in urban communities.
In achieving our objective, we connect the people to affordable assets as well as empowering building contractors providing access to improved resources (inclusive of building materials, training on best building practices, sales of properties for higher value) to boost the quality and quantity of housing in Nigeria and also provide a healthy profit after sales or let for both contractor and sponsor.
We are a team of passionate men and women between the ages of 25 and 40, who have come from the Building, Finance and Technology sector. Our common goal is to seek for ways to reduce the cost of accommodation on urban settlers, contribute to the increase in affordable housing , and to ensure more youth participation in Real Estate.
